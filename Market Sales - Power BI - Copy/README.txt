# 📊 Power BI Sales Dashboard  

## 📌 Overview  
This is my first **Power BI Data Visualization Project**, analyzing sales trends, customer behaviors, and product performance. 
The dashboard provides insights into revenue, product sales, and customer preferences.  

## 🚀 Features  
- 📅 **Sales Trends Analysis** (Revenue by day, hour, and month)  
- 👥 **Customer Insights** (Gender, Membership Type, Payment Method)  
- 🛒 **Top-Selling Product Lines** (Identifies the most profitable categories)  
- 📈 **COGS vs. Gross Income Comparison**  
- 🔍 **Dynamic visualizations with interactive filters**

## 📊 Key Insights  
- 🏆 **Top-selling product lines**: Food & Beverages, Sports & Travel, Electronics Accessories.  
- 💳 **Most common payment method**: E-wallets.  
- 📈 **Best sales days**: Weekends have higher sales than weekdays.  
- 📉 **Least profitable product line**: Health and Beauty
